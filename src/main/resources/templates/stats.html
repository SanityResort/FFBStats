Each table shows a certain portion of the dice rolls of a team. See below for details in what each portion includes. The heading of each table also includes the total amout of results listed.

Some stats differ from FUMBBL match reports. Frenzy follow up blocks are reported as separate blocks, where as FUMBBL counts the block action. Stab and Chainsaw blocks are not counted as blocks, only the armour/injury rolls are.

Total D6s
All D6 roll, 1D6 and 2D6 but no block dice.
Single D6s
Includes all single D6 rolls, e.g. dodges, pick ups, catches etc.
Armour Rolls
Includes every armour roll against any player of the opposing team, including blocks, rerolled armour rolls from "Piling on", failed dodges etc.
Injury Rolls
Includes every injury roll against any player of the opposing team, including injuries from "Throw a Rock" and rerolled injuries from "Piling on".
Total 2D6s
All 2D6 rolls.
Other 2D6s
All 2D6 rolls that are not armour or injury rolls, including Fame and escape rolls against players with Tentacles or Shadowing.
Total Blocks
Shows how many blocks have been thrown by this team, split according to the amount of dice used. This does also include rerolled blocks.
Rerolled Blocks
Shows how many blocks have been rerolled, split according to the amount of dice used.
Successful Blocks
Shows how many blocks knocked down the defender, split according to the amount of dice used. This also includes blocks knocking down the attacker as well.
Failed Blocks
Shows how many blocks knocked down the attacker, split according to the amount of dice used. This also includes blocks knocking down the defender as well.
Total Block Dice
All block dice rolled by the team. Remember the push result is twice as common as any others.
